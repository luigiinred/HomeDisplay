Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At Aerial Picture Frame, we are committed to protecting your privacy above all else. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy-first approach and how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect absolutely no information about you. Aerial Picture Frame is designed with privacy as the core principle. Here's what this means:

No Account Required

  • No Registration: You do not need to create an account to use Aerial Picture Frame. Simply download and start using the app immediately.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information.
  • No Login Required: There are no usernames, passwords, or authentication systems.

Local-Only Operation

  • Device-Only Storage: All your photos, calendar data, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store any of your data on our servers or in the cloud.
  • No Data Transmission: Your photos and personal data never leave your device.
  • No Analytics: We do not track how you use the app or collect any usage statistics.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics
  • No location data
  • No network information
  • No crash reports
  • No performance metrics
  • No user behavior data

How We Use Your Information

We don't use your information because we don't collect any. Since all data remains on your device, we have no access to your information and therefore cannot use it for any purpose.

What this means for you:

  • Complete Control: You have full control over your photos and personal data at all times
  • No Data Mining: We cannot analyze, sell, or share your data because we don't have it
  • No Targeted Advertising: We cannot create profiles or target you with ads
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Since we don't collect data, there's nothing to share with third parties

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We cannot share your information because we don't collect any. Since all your data stays on your device, there is no information for us to share with anyone.

This means:

  • No Data Sharing: We cannot share your data with service providers, advertisers, or any third parties
  • No Legal Disclosure: We cannot be compelled to disclose your data because we don't have it
  • No Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, there is no data to transfer
  • No Government Requests: We cannot comply with government data requests because we don't have any data

Data Security

Your data is already secure because it never leaves your device. Since we don't collect, store, or transmit any of your information, there is no data for us to secure on our end.

Security benefits of our approach:

  • No Data Breaches: We cannot be hacked because we don't store your data
  • No Server Vulnerabilities: Your data isn't on our servers, so it can't be compromised
  • No Network Interception: We don't transmit your data, so it can't be intercepted
  • Device-Level Security: Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features

Data Retention

We don't retain any data because we don't collect any. All your information remains on your device under your complete control. You can delete the app at any time, and all your data will be removed with it.

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data because it never leaves your device. Since we don't collect any information, traditional privacy rights don't apply, but you have even greater control:

  • Complete Ownership: You own and control 100% of your data at all times
  • Instant Deletion: Delete the app and all your data is gone immediately
  • No Data Requests: You don't need to request your data because it's already on your device
  • No Opt-Out Needed: There's nothing to opt out of because we don't collect anything
  • Device-Level Control: Use your device's privacy settings to control app permissions

Third-Party Services

Aerial Picture Frame may connect to your photo libraries and calendar services, but this communication happens directly between your device and these services. We do not act as an intermediary or collect any data from these interactions.

Important points about third-party integrations:

  • Direct Communication: Your device communicates directly with your photo libraries and calendar services
  • No Data Interception: We don't intercept, store, or process any data from these communications
  • Your Device's Privacy: The privacy of these communications is controlled by your device's settings and the individual service providers
  • No Third-Party Data Collection: We don't share any data with service providers because we don't collect any

Children's Privacy

We don't collect information from anyone, including children. Since our app doesn't collect any personal information from users of any age, children's privacy is inherently protected. There is no data for us to collect, store, or process from children or adults.

Privacy protection for all users:

  • No Age Verification: We don't need to verify age because we don't collect data
  • No Parental Consent: No parental consent is needed because no data is collected
  • Universal Privacy: The same privacy protections apply to users of all ages
  • No Special Handling: Children's data doesn't require special handling because we don't have any

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@aerialpictureframe.app

Website: aerialpictureframe.app

Support: Contact Support
